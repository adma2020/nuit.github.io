About ADMA

The 16th International Conference on Advanced Data Mining and Applications(ADMA) On 12-15 November 2020 in Foshan, China

  • Home   /  
  • About ADMA

The premiere International Conference series on Advanced Data Mining and Applications aims at bringing together the experts on data mining from all over the world, and provides an international forum for the dissemination of original research results in data mining, spanning applications, algorithms, software and systems, and different applied disciplines with potentials in data mining. The proceedings of the conference are published by Springer in its Lecture Notes in ComputerScience series.

The ADMA can be chased 13 years ago. The first conference was held in Wuhan, China，in 2005. And the 2nd in Xian, China, in 2006; 3rd in Harbin, China, in 2007; 4th in Chengdu, China, in 2008; 5th in Beijing, China, in 2009; 6th in Chongqing, China, in 2010; 7th in Beijing, China, in 2011; 8th in Nanjing, China, in 2012; 9th in Hangzhou, China, in 2013; 10th in Guilin, China, in 2014; 12th in Gold Coast, Australia, in 2016; 13th in Singapore, in 2017. The past proceedings can be accessed from Springer .

Example acceptance rates of ADMA:
25 regular (4%) and 75 papers from 602 submissions in its debut in 2005
32 regular (14%) and 64 short papers from 222 submissions in 2013
18 spotlight (17%) and 38 papers from 105 submissions in 2016

Citations of ADMA papers:
Average citations for ADMA papers over 960 papers (including short and demo papers) is around 6.
Top 3 most-cited ADMA papers: 97 (2006), 83 (2005), 76 (2007). 
ADMA proceedings are among the most downloaded ones in Springer. For example, ADMA’05 was downloaded 108,318 times, and ADMA’16 was downloaded 17,066 times.
