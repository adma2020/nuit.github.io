ID Inquiry
  • Enter the email address you used when you registered and click on the ID Inquiry button.
  • Your Login ID will be sent to the registered email address.
  • Only a representative can make an ID inquiry.
  • Registered users can retrieve their login accounts through the E-mail address they filled in when registering.
Password Reissue
  • Enter the email address and Login ID you used when you registered and click on the Password Reissue button.
  • A temporary password will be sent to the registered email address.
  • Only a representative can have a password reissued.
  • The password can be reset by entering the E-mail and login account when registering.
